Who is Jesus Christ and how can getting to know Him benefit us?

Coming to know Jesus Christ as the central figure in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints brings profound benefits, including increased faith, hope, and peace, as well as the ability to endure life's challenges with greater strength and understanding. It also fosters a deeper relationship with Heavenly Father and provides guidance and direction in daily life.
